Karu's Beatstore

Technologies: React, Redux, react-bootstrap, Python | Django-rest, WaveSurfer.js, PostgreSQL, AWS | S3 & RDBS

Karu's Beatstore was made with the intention of making it easier for producers and musicians with the intention to sell there instrumentals. One aspect i've observed over the past couple years within up and coming musicians, is that most of the time they may be promoting their products through Instagram or other social media apps, withthe usage of venmo or cashapp. I made this undertaking to show that a internet site can make it simpler for both the consumer and the owner of the track, so as to effortlessly make a transaction and avoid being scammed.

Space Exploration

Technologies: React, SCSS, Nasa API, Arcgis 4.18 JS API, WOW.js

Space exploration being prevalent as ever with companies such as SpaceX and Nasa the interest for some people has never been higher. Using the new 3D sceneview feature of the Arcgis 4.18 layer rendering api. I thought it would be cool to make a space exploring themed website where the user gets a daily article from Nasa, and can explore around The moon, mars and mercury and be able to add different layers such as Potentional SpaceX landing sites on mars or the craters on mercury, the moon or mars.

Cryptory

Technologies: HTML, CSS | SCSS, JavaScript, HighCharts.js, Coinstats API

Cryptory is a crypotcurrency tracking application that presents the overall market cap, overall volume in 24h, BTC dominance. For each currency it displayed the 24h change, current price, the price converted to bitcoin, 24h volume as well as a price graph for the past 7 days. Also latest news stories from credible sources revolving around the crypotcurrency market.

Teenage Engineering Demo

Technologies: React, Redux, Tailwind CSS, C# ASP.NET Core, PostgreSQL, Redis, Stripe (Payment)

This Teenage Engineering Demo is a full-stack eCommerce website built out based upon the company Teenage Engineerings website design and use cases. This website is a fully functioning demo with a store that you can add items into a customer cart using redis, going to checkout and pay via the stripe api.
